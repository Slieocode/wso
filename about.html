A free entry point for some of the most valuable prize draws on the Internet

Omnis Media/TA Wesendoffers.co.uk is a UK company connecting consumers with information and offers. The website itself, acts as a free entry point for some of the most valuable prize draws on the Internet.

Upon full completion of the sign-up form, Omnis Media T/A Wesendoffers.co.uk will give you, t an entry to win your selected prize. It’s as simple as that! Each time you submit a completed questionnaire, you will automatically enter the relevant, sponsored prize draw. To be entered into your selected prize draw, you only need to complete the registration page once however, you can enter as many prize draws as you wish. GOOD LUCK!

Omnis Media T/A Wesendoffers.co.uk have a business model that we feel is simple. You enter the information required on each form fill, we select winners and following this, everyone who enters will updates on offers that we feel will be of interest to you, as the customer – Easy, right? We generate revenue, by allowing selected partners to access your information to promote relevant offers. This model is a value exchange, to be able to give products away as part of our free prize draws. This means that when you submit your details through the prize draw questionnaires, you do so on the understanding that you will receive offers (from Omnis Media, its sponsors and its clients) based on the information you have provided. Wesendoffers.co.uk, its clients and its sponsors, may also use the address details provided on the registration page, to send you offers or information relevant to you through the post. If you prefer not to receive such offers from Wesendoffers.co.uk or its clients / sponsors, please contact us at takemeout@omnismedia.co.uk. There, you can either amend or remove your details. You can also amend your details or even de-register at any time and to do this, simply sign–in at the top of the webpage and select the 'My Account' link.

We do hope this information gives you a clear understanding of the site and, what you’re signing up for. To make our business work we believe in full transparency and the above, sets out to offer that. If you have any questions and/or would like to speak to a member of the Omnis Media / Wesendoffers.co.uk team, please contact us on 01422 849600. Thanks.
